# TTRPG System Reference & FAQ

Welcome to the Global Campaign Hub. This reference document provides descriptions and official resource links for all recognized tabletop roleplaying systems available in our system picker directory. Each system uses a stable **slug** identifier (for example, `dnd-5e`, `pathfinder-2e`) stored on your campaign.

If your game system is not listed below, select **"Other"** in your Campaign Settings to input a custom ruleset.

---

## Tactical & High-Fantasy

### 13th Age
* **Description:** A dynamic, d20-powered fantasy system designed by former D&D lead developers that blends tactical grid combat with narrative rules like "One Unique Thing" and background-based skills.
* **Resources:** [Official Pelgrane Press Site](https://pelgranepress.com/13th-age/)

### Dungeons & Dragons (5th Edition)
* **Slug:** `dnd-5e`
* **Description:** The world's most recognized and widely played TTRPG, utilizing a d20-based system centered on heroic high-fantasy, class progression, and tactical combat.
* **Resources:** [Official D&D Beyond Portal](https://www.dndbeyond.com/)

### Daggerheart
* **Slug:** `daggerheart`
* **Description:** A fantasy RPG designed for streaming-style play, blending narrative collaboration with tactical combat and hope/fear mechanics.
* **Resources:** [Daggerheart Official Site](https://www.daggerheart.com/)

### Dragonbane
* **Description:** A fast, brutally fun, and highly accessible Scandinavian fantasy RPG built on a d20 roll-under mechanic that favors quick play, dangerous encounters, and open-ended exploration.
* **Resources:** [Free League Publishing](https://freeleaguepublishing.com/games/dragonbane/)

### Fabula Ultima
* **Slug:** `fabula-ultima`
* **Description:** A "tabletop JRPG" designed to emulate the feel of console roleplaying games with class combos, limit breaks, and collaborative storytelling.
* **Resources:** [Need Games — Fabula Ultima](https://www.needgames.it/fabula-ultima/)

### Pathfinder (1e)
* **Description:** Built on the foundations of 3.5e D&D, this legacy system is celebrated for offering massive mechanical depth, unparalleled character-building options, and precise tactical depth.
* **Resources:** [Paizo Pathfinder Portal](https://paizo.com/pathfinder)

### Pathfinder (2e)
* **Description:** A highly streamlined, modernized version of the tactical ruleset featuring a robust 3-action economy system, deep but balanced modular character customization, and incredibly tight combat design.
* **Resources:** [Archives of Nethys (Official PRD)](https://2e.aonprd.com/)

### Pendragon
* **Slug:** `pendragon`
* **Description:** A legendary game centered on Arthurian legend and long-form character growth across generations of knights and their families.
* **Resources:** [Chaosium — Pendragon](https://www.chaosium.com/pendragon/)

### Shadowdark RPG
* **Description:** An acclaimed Old School Renaissance (OSR) engine with modern intuitive sensibilities, featuring real-time light tracking, roll-to-cast magic, and lethal dungeon-crawling focus.
* **Resources:** [The Arcane Library](https://www.thearcanelibrary.com/pages/shadowdark)

### Twilight Sword
* **Description:** A dark, moody tactical fantasy system that weaves themes of fading magical eras, historical melancholy, and high-lethality sword combat.
* **Resources:** [Indie RPG Marketplace](https://itch.io/physical-games/tag-ttrpg)

### Warhammer Fantasy Roleplay
* **Description:** A dark, grim-dark d100 career-based fantasy system where players take on mundane professions (like rat-catchers or boatmen) trying to survive an unforgiving, chaotic world.
* **Resources:** [Cubicle 7 Games](https://cubicle7games.com/our-games/warhammer-fantasy-roleplay-2)

## Sci-Fi, Mechs & Cyberpunk

### Alien: The Roleplaying Game
* **Description:** A cinematic sci-fi horror game leveraging a specialized Year Zero Engine d6 pool. Players balance stress mechanics that enhance performance right up until a character panics.
* **Resources:** [Alien RPG Official Site](https://alien-rpg.com/)

### Cyberpunk Red
* **Description:** The official prequel timeline to the *Cyberpunk 2077* video game. It delivers deadly Interlock-system street-level combat, netrunning, and corporate dystopia.
* **Resources:** [R. Talsorian Games](https://rtalsoriangames.com/cyberpunk-red/)

### Lancer
* **Description:** An exceptional blend of deep tactical, grid-based mech combat and narrative, rules-light downtime operations set in a vibrant, mud-and-lasers sci-fi universe.
* **Resources:** [Massif Press](https://massifpress.com/)

### Neon Odyssey: Space Opera
* **Description:** A high-action, dramatic sci-fi roleplaying engine engineered for fast ship-to-ship skirmishes, galactic exploration, and epic space opera campaign arcs.
* **Resources:** [Indie RPG Marketplace](https://itch.io/physical-games/tag-ttrpg)

### Shadowrun
* **Slug:** `shadowrun`
* **Description:** A unique blend of high-fantasy (magic/elves) and cyberpunk (hackers/tech) in a dystopian near-future setting.
* **Resources:** [Shadowrun Tabletop](https://www.shadowruntabletop.com/)

### Starfinder (1e)
* **Description:** A science-fantasy ruleset derived from Pathfinder 1e that meshes high-tech spaceships, cyberware, and futuristic weaponry with classic magical fantasy elements.
* **Resources:** [Paizo Starfinder Hub](https://paizo.com/starfinder)

### Starfinder (2e)
* **Description:** A complete modernization of the science-fantasy ruleset, completely rebuilt to seamlessly cross-play with the updated tactical framework of the *Pathfinder 2e* d20 engine.
* **Resources:** [Paizo Starfinder Playtest & Development](https://paizo.com/starfinder)

### Star Trek Adventures
* **Description:** Powered by the 2d20 system, this narrative-forward game models Starfleet operations, teamwork, ethical dilemmas, and ship management down to the individual department.
* **Resources:** [Modiphius Entertainment](https://www.modiphius.net/pages/star-trek-adventures)

### Traveller
* **Description:** A historic, gold-standard d6 hard-sci-fi sandbox system famous for its deep starship design, trade mechanics, and highly immersive, potentially lethal character life-path generation.
* **Resources:** [Mongoose Publishing](https://www.mongoosepublishing.com/)

## Horror & Urban Fantasy

### Call of Cthulhu
* **Description:** A d100 investigative system centered on cosmic horror, mystery solving, and a delicate sanity tracking system where exposing your mind to the mythos risks permanent madness.
* **Resources:** [Chaosium Inc.](https://www.chaosium.com/call-of-cthulhu-rpg/)

### City of Mist
* **Description:** A gorgeous, cinematic detective noir game where regular citizens channel the mythical powers of legends, gods, and fairy tales using tag-based narrative mechanics.
* **Resources:** [Son of Oak Game Studio](https://cityofmist.co/)

### Delta Green
* **Description:** An award-winning, spin-off evolution of the Cthulhu Mythos mutated into a modern-day conspiracy thriller about secret government black-ops agents keeping the unexplainable quiet.
* **Resources:** [Delta Green Official Site](https://www.deltagreen.org/)

### Vampire: The Masquerade
* **Description:** The flagship title of the Storyteller system, placing players in the shoes of secret undead factions struggling with politics, personal horror, and keeping the "Beast" at bay.
* **Resources:** [World of Darkness Official Hub](https://www.worldofdarkness.com/)

## LitRPG & Adapted Worlds

### Avatar Legends
* **Description:** The official Powered by the Apocalypse tabletop game allowing players to tell heartfelt stories of balance, martial arts, and elemental bending across the eras of the Avatar universe.
* **Resources:** [Magpie Games](https://magpiegames.com/pages/avatargetting)

### Cosmere RPG (Mistborn)
* **Description:** Built on an original d20-based hybrid narrative system by Brotherwise Games, this campaign setting allows players to burn metals via Allomancy and explore Scadrial across both major eras.
* **Resources:** [Brotherwise Games Cosmere Hub](https://www.brotherwisegames.com/)

### Discworld: Adventures in Ankh-Morpork
* **Description:** Powered by the bespoke, rules-light "Narrativium System," this Modiphius game captures the exact wit and humanistic satire of Sir Terry Pratchett's legendary city.
* **Resources:** [Modiphius Entertainment Discworld Portal](https://modiphius.net/pages/discworld-adventures)

### Fallout RPG
* **Description:** The official adaptation of the post-apocalyptic video game series utilizing the 2d20 system, complete with scavenging tables, VATS tactical combat, and retro-futuristic survival mechanics.
* **Resources:** [Modiphius Entertainment Fallout Line](https://www.modiphius.net/pages/fallout-the-roleplaying-game)

### The One Ring
* **Description:** An elegant, highly immersive system designed specifically to capture the tone, travel fatigue, fellowship bonds, and creeping shadow found in J.R.R. Tolkien’s Middle-earth.
* **Resources:** [Free League Publishing](https://freeleaguepublishing.com/games/the-one-ring/)

## Narrative, Powered by the Apocalypse (PbtA) & Queer Indie Indie

### Apocalypse World: Burned Over
* **Description:** A streamlined, re-designed, non-explicit edition of the legendary post-apocalyptic game that established the "Powered by the Apocalypse" framework.
* **Resources:** [Lumpley Games](http://lumpley.com/)

### Blades in the Dark
* **Description:** A landmark heist engine tracking a crew of daring scoundrels building a criminal enterprise in a haunted, industrial-fantasy city fueled by lightning barriers and ghost blood.
* **Resources:** [Evil Hat Productions](https://evilhat.com/product/blades-in-the-dark/)

### Blue Rose
* **Description:** The definitive "romantic fantasy" roleplaying game focusing on cooperation, animal companions, complex social intrigue, and inclusive heroic storytelling.
* **Resources:** [Green Ronin Publishing](https://greenronin.com/bluerose/)

### Dream Askew
* **Description:** A highly influential, diceless, GM-less game focusing on a queer enclave trying to survive and find community amid a crumbling post-apocalyptic backdrop.
* **Resources:** [Buried Without Ceremony](https://buriedwithoutceremony.com/dream-askew)

### Girl by Moonlight
* **Description:** A striking, Forged in the Dark mechanical adaptation explicitly designed to explore the struggles, bonds, and defiant hope found in the magical girl anime genre.
* **Resources:** [Evil Hat Productions](https://evilhat.com/product/girl-by-moonlight/)

### Glitter Hearts
* **Description:** An action-packed, collaborative PbtA magical girl game that lets players construct a dual-identity hero, balancing mundane high school life with neon team superpower fights.
* **Resources:** [Levesque Games](https://www.levesquegames.com/)

### Monsterhearts
* **Description:** A critical darling PbtA game that explores the messy, volatile, and deeply emotional lives of teenage monsters grappling with adolescent desire and social isolation.
* **Resources:** [Buried Without Ceremony](https://buriedwithoutceremony.com/monsterhearts)

### Powered by the Apocalypse (PbtA)
* **Description:** Not a single game, but a flexible fiction-first design philosophy and engine utilizing descriptive "Moves" triggered directly by in-character roleplay.
* **Resources:** [PbtA World Directory](http://apocalypse-world.com/pbta/)

### Thirsty Sword Lesbians
* **Description:** A celebration of queer representation, focusing on high-melodrama sword duels, passionate romance, systemic rebellion, and emotional redemptions.
* **Resources:** [Evil Hat Productions](https://evilhat.com/product/thirsty-sword-lesbians/)

## Genre-Agnostic Sandbox Tools

### Cypher System
* **Description:** A fast-paced, rules-light pool engine developed by Monte Cook Games that heavily emphasizes asset exploration, player player-driven resource spend, and GM flexibility.
* **Resources:** [Monte Cook Games](https://www.montecookgames.com/store/product/cypher-system-rulebook-2/)

### FATE
* **Description:** A brilliantly simple narrative game system that completely replaces traditional numerical attributes with player-invented descriptive "Aspects" and a highly collaborative narrative token economy.
* **Resources:** [Evil Hat Productions](https://evilhat.com/product/fate-core/)

### GURPS
* **Description:** The *Generic Universal RolePlaying System*. A legendary, highly modular d6 simulator designed to mathematically accommodate absolutely any genre or cross-epoch game style imaginable.
* **Resources:** [Steve Jackson Games](http://www.sjgames.com/gurps/)

### Savage Worlds
* **Description:** A swingy, pulse-pounding explosion-die tactical system designed around a fast, cinematic mantra that lets Game Masters seamlessly spin up any pulp genre quickly.
* **Resources:** [Pinnacle Entertainment Group](https://peginc.com/savage-worlds/)

## Frequently Asked Questions

#### Q: How do these choices affect my wiki space?
A: Your game system selection is stored as campaign metadata for discovery, filtering, and display. Per-system sidebar presets and stat-block templates are planned for a future release; all campaigns currently receive the same core wiki structure, customizable dashboards, tag connections, and quest tracking tools regardless of system.

#### Q: My game system isn't listed here. What should I do?
A: Don't panic! Simply head to **Campaign Settings > General**, select **"Other"** from the Game System field, and write your system's name. You will retain all core wiki structural tools, customizable dashboards, tag connections, and quest tracking databases.
